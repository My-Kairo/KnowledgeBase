Email requirements

No email are to be transfered to the BO queues. Only email are to be transfered to the Change of Tenancy (COT) queue if the email is related to COT request. If an email is received the entire chain needs to be reviewed and the appropriate action being taken in line with the process. All email need to be worked accordingly and dispositioned in line with action taken to be removed from the queue as completed.

If you are due for break, break status must be requested as follows:

  • 5 or more in your queue - Aux break 10 mins prior
  • 4 or more in a queue - Aux break 8 mins prior
  • 3 or less in a queue - Aux break 6 mins prior

If an email is received into the incorrect email box and that email can be worked, then the agent will be expected to work the email and disposition it per the action taken. if the email cannot be worked, due to the email not populating the account the and the email is for the termination box and LOA box then the agent is expected to send no auth/third party email.
